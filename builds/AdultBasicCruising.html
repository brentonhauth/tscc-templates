HOW TO FIND US

We are located at 1391 Lake Shore Boulevard West, in behind the Royal Canadian Legion and just east of the Boulevard Club. Access the parking lot by turning onto Net Drive which is a small lane off of Lake Shore Boulevard in between the tennis courts and outdoor fitness area. You do not need to pay for parking for drop offs, if you're parking for longer periods of time free parking passes are available upon arrival. Bike racks are available inside the gate. The gate code will be provided via email before your course begins.

The closest transit stop is the Dowling Avenue stop via the 504 King Streetcar. Walk south down Dowling to the pedestrian bridge across the Gardiner, and cross Lake Shore at the lights.
WHAT TO BRING

For your Basic Cruising course you will need the following:

  • Lifejacket
  • Closed toed shoes
  • A refillable waterbottle
  • A lunch (if you are doing a weekend course; there are also fast food restaurants nearby)
  • A pen/pencil and a notebook are highly recommended
  • Hat, sunglasses, sunscreen and/or other forms of sun protection
  • A wind/water resistant jacket
